FAQ

Is BabyCities only useful in one city?
No. The app is designed to work across neighborhoods, cities, and travel situations wherever parents contribute practical local knowledge.
What makes it different from normal place discovery?
BabyCities focuses on practical family details like changing tables, baby chairs, stroller access, and play areas instead of generic venue reviews.
Can parents improve the map?
Yes. Parents can validate amenities and share practical knowledge, making the app more reliable for the next family.
