Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

BabyCities collects only the information needed to run the service, improve the app experience, and help parents find practical family-friendly places more easily.

What may be collected

  • Basic account information like name and email.
  • Content you add inside the app, such as place details or updates.
  • Usage, device, and location-related information depending on settings.

How it may be used

  • To provide the service and make results more relevant.
  • To improve the app and support users.
  • To keep practical place information useful for families.

What users should know

Some information shared in the app may be visible to other users. The goal is to make BabyCities more useful, while handling data responsibly and as clearly as possible.
