Using BabyCities.

Here is the simple version: BabyCities is meant to help parents find practical family-friendly places and share useful information with each other in a respectful and honest way.

What users agree to

  • Use the app in a lawful and respectful way.
  • Share practical information that is as accurate as possible.
  • Keep account access private and secure.
  • Not misuse the service, copy its data, or publish misleading content.

What BabyCities can do

  • Improve, update, suspend, or change parts of the service over time.
  • Use shared content to operate and improve the app.
  • Remove abusive, false, or harmful content when necessary.

Important note

BabyCities is designed to make outings easier, but places can change. Parents should still use their own judgment when one amenity is essential for the moment.
